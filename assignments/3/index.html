About Me

Hello! My name is Vireak, or just V. I am an international student from Cambodia pursuing a degree in Computer Science with a minor in Mathematics. I am very interested in Computer Graphics and 3D Rendering, I love learning about the clever algorithms in rendering as well as the advanced Mathematics used in those algorithms.

I aspire to study abroad in Germany one day, perhaps even pursure a Master's there and do research Computer Graphics. I really want to experience Europe for the first time.

Outside of university, I volunteer in high school classrooms as a tutor. I love teaching Math and Physics. I want to share my passion and interest to other students and be a source of inspiration for students.

I really like learning languages. After I am proficient in German, I would love to maybe learn Spanish or Japanese. One of the reasons that I wanted to learn these languages is becuase I enjoy listening to music in these languages. I like music and I want to be able to play the guitar and write my own songs one day.

I also like art. I used to do pixel art back when I played Minecraft a lot and wanted to modify the game with addons. Nowadays I want to get into digital art and learn to express complex emotions in my art pieces.
